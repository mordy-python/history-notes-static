Wilmot Proviso

He proposed to congress that New Mexico, & California should be free Southerners cried fowl

Popular Sovereignty

  • Its the idea that territories should determine whether or not they were slave states or free states
  • It seemed like a good compromise between Abolitionists and Slave Supporters

James K. Polk decided not to run for a second term in 1848 The Democrats nominated Lewis Cask to run for President. He supported Popular Sovereignty This is the last time the Whigs nominated a candidate The Whigs nominated Zachary Taylor He was a general who fought in the Mexican American War He owned slaves A breakaway party called the Free Soil Party formed. It was made of Dems and Whigs who liked Pop. Sovereignty but didnt like the candidate * They were strongly against slavery * The Republican party formed out of them

Taylor won the election of 1848 Gold was discovered in 1848 in California It started the Gold Rush The miners brought violence * They fought and killed to steal other miners gold And disease This caused lots of tension Eventually the miners overran Californias small communities The Californians decided they wanted to be a state They petitioned Congress for statehood without becoming a territory first They drafted a state constitution * It clearly banned slavery in California * This was revolutionary * Because of this the Southerners didn't allow it to become a state * It was left in limbo just like Texas

Harriet Tubman

  • She was an escaped slave
  • She went back to the south as a spy during the civil war
  • After the War she helped slaves escape through the Underground RailRoad
    • She led them to safe houses and they traveled all the way north to Canada or south to Haiti
